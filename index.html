About the Project

iProViS is a cutting-edge product recognition system designed to provide users with detailed product information, price comparisons, recyclability insights, and more. This project aims to revolutionize shopping experiences while contributing to sustainability and eco-friendly practices.

Features

Product Recognition

Capture product photos to retrieve detailed information.

Price Comparison

Compare prices across multiple stores in seconds.

Recyclability Information

Learn whether the product or its packaging is recyclable.

Multilingual Support

View product details in your preferred language.
