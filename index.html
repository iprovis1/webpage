About the Project

iProViS (Intelligent Product Vision System) is an innovative solution designed to revolutionize the way users interact with everyday products. Leveraging advanced computer vision and artificial intelligence, the application enables users to retrieve detailed product information simply by taking a photo. Whether you’re shopping in a supermarket, comparing prices online, or checking for eco-friendly packaging, iProViS brings all the essential information to your fingertips.

The app provides comprehensive details, including current prices, stock availability, and price comparisons across multiple stores, both online and offline. Additionally, iProViS promotes environmental awareness by offering insights into the recyclability of products and their packaging. With multilingual support, users from diverse linguistic backgrounds can access product details seamlessly in their preferred language.

By combining cutting-edge technology with user-friendly features, iProViS enhances the shopping experience while contributing to sustainable and eco-conscious consumption. Whether you are a savvy shopper looking for the best deals or an environmentally conscious user seeking greener alternatives, iProViS empowers you to make informed decisions with ease and confidence.

Features

Product Recognition

This project aims to revolutionize the retail experience by leveraging computer vision and deep learning technologies to replace traditional barcodes and QR codes for product recognition in sectors such as supermarkets, clothing, food, and electronics. The developed system addresses multiple consumer needs, including effortless access to product price information, comparative price analysis across stores, and multilingual support for accessing detailed product content information.

Price Comparison

With İProViS, the "Price Comparison" feature enables users to compare product prices across sources in an easy way. Utilizing computer vision, it can scan a product's barcode or analyze its image for instant retrieval of pricing from online stores or physical retailers. This feature allows users to find the best deals to make their shopping fast and efficient. Users can now make budget-friendly decisions using real-time price analysis.

Recyclability Information

The product is designed as a standalone mobile application integrated with the iPRoVis system's API for advanced functionality. This integration enables not only product identification but also facilitates an intelligent recycling process by recognizing recyclable packaging types, determining whether the packaging is empty or full, and providing weight details. This functionality ensures efficient sorting and recognition by smart recycling machines, thereby reducing the time and effort required for waste disposal and recycling.

Multilingual Support

İProViS’s Multilingual feature makes it easier for users across the globe as it provides support for more than one language. This feature allows the software to read product details, labels, and pricing in other languages employing state of the art computer vision and natural language processing (NLP) technologies. Customers can communicate with the application in their language of choice, therefore improving the function and usefulness of the software. Whether you’re purchasing items from a local store or ordering from abroad, İProVis changes according to the languages you required, hence it becomes a universal shopping assistant.
